## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I share multiple portfolios?
**A:** Yes! You can share as many portfolios as you want. Each gets its own unique link.

### Q: Will my share link change if I unshare and re-share?
**A:** No, the same token is used, so the link remains the same.

### Q: Can I edit a shared portfolio?
**A:** Yes, you can load and edit it. However, you'll need to save it again to update the shared version.

### Q: How do I know if my portfolio is public?
**A:** Public portfolios show "ğŸ”— Public (X views)" in your "My Portfolios" list.

### Q: Can I delete a shared portfolio?
**A:** Yes, deleting a portfolio will also remove its public access.

### Q: Is there a limit to how many people can view my portfolio?
**A:** No, there's no limit. Share with as many people as you'd like!

### Q: Can I customize the share URL?
**A:** Currently, share URLs use randomly generated tokens. Custom URLs may be added in future updates.
